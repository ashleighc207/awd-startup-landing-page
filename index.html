    Get Heik'n

    Heik it wherever you leik it

    Tired of the local park or strolls around the neighborhood, but don't know where to go? Us, too. That's why we created Heik - a hub to explore new places with genuine feedback from real people. With detailed descriptions, hours, locations, and so much more, your next Heik has never been so easy!

    What is a Heik?

    A Heik is a specific destination - ranging from the lowest valley to the highest peak. Each Heik comes with basic information about the destination, including (the most important part) level of pet-friendly!

    • Pet Approval

      So what if you like the Heik, all that truly matters is that your furry (or not so furry, we don't judge!) friend can enjoy the Heik with you. Our pet approval stamp weighs factors such as available doggy bags, water fill up stations, breed restrictions, and many more!

    • Meetup

      Heiking alone can be a peaceful time to collect your thoughts. On the other hand, you can open the app and see Heik'rs near you that want to meet up! Explore new places while making new friends along the way. Plus, who else will understand your wilderness horror stories?

    • 3-Factor Rating

      In addition to our Pet Approval stamp, we also rate our parks using a 3-factor weighted rating. Our rating includes what the community has rated it as, what our team has rated it as, and an objective review of all facilities, activities, and even accessibility.

    • Heik Mobile App

      Maybe you enjoy planning your trips online, but, let's be real. When you're on a Heik, it's just you, the wilderness, and your cellphone. Open up the Heik app while on a Heik to see where the nearest bathroom is, which trail you're on, how far you have Heik'd, and more!
